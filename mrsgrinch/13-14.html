a cookie story

This year will be the third I'll be sending that boy his chocolate chips cookies at his college far away. And it will be the first year I'll be sending his brother his batch of cookies but to a different far away college.

It'll be the first time in 20 plus years that no one will be coming in from school saying, “I smell cookies or I don't smell cookies mommm!”

this year only my husband and I will celebrate a move and a new house with those cookies.

There is a new generation, three grand babies with a fourth on the way to make THE chocolate chip cookies for when they come to visit.
