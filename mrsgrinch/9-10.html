a cookie story

THERE WERE NO COOKIES.

I had put a load of whites to soak in the washer and had set the timer to remind me to begin a wash cycle.

I wanted, with all my heart to wiggle my nose and make cookies appear in that oven, but instead I started to babble “Oh baby I'm so sorry there aren't any cookies.”
