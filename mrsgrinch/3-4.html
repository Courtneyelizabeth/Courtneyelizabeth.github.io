A Cookie Story

THE cookies

I was making chocolate chip cookies today and like always I got out the cook book that has THE recipe, got the bowls down and proceeded the cookie baking ritual.

How many batches have I made over the last 20 plus years? Hundreds?

And no I don't need the book anymore but I just like reading the recipe again. I love the book it is full of great recipes and memories.

These chocolate chip cookies have been an institution, a ritual.
