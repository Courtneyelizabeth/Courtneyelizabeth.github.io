THE cookies

There was the time 3-year-old Alex (he is number 4 in a line of 5 kids) was in the bath tub, the timer on my oven went off and he was out of the tub in a flash.

Bubbles and puddles gave evidence to where he was heading.

When I caught up with him he was standing next to the oven, holding his little arm straight up in order to keep my oven mitt on.

He had an expectant look on his face. It hit me as I reached over the burners to turn off the timer, he was waiting for cookies.
