a cookie story

I opened the door and he peered into the dark cold depths of the oven.

He leaned in trying to see deeper into the oven.

Still no cookies.

He straightened up, gave me a look I couldn't quite read,
opened the drawer next to the oven pulled the mitt off, threw it in the drawer pushed the drawer shut shot me another look and ran out of the kitchen leaving me staring at the puddle he created.
